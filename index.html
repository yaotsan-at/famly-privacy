隱私權政策 Privacy Policy

最後更新 Last Updated：2026-05-31
家小事不收集、不傳送任何使用者資料至開發者伺服器。所有資料僅存於您的裝置及您個人的 iCloud 私有資料庫。

1. 總覽

家小事（Famly）是一款家庭記錄管理工具，協助家長記錄家人的健康、車輛、證件、寵物及孩子的成長資訊。本 app 由個人開發者發布，不隸屬於任何公司。

本隱私政策說明我們如何處理您的個人資料。

2. 我們收集什麼資料？

我們不收集任何資料。所有您在 app 中輸入的內容，均儲存於您自己的裝置或您個人的 Apple iCloud 私有資料庫，開發者無法存取。
家庭記錄

健康紀錄、車輛保養、證件到期、孩子成長、寵物資料等，均透過 Apple 的 CloudKit 私有資料庫同步，僅您本人可存取。

帳密保險箱

密碼明文儲存於裝置的 iOS Keychain（硬體加密），或透過 iCloud Keychain 同步至您的家庭成員。密碼不會上傳至任何第三方伺服器。查看密碼需通過 Face ID / Touch ID 驗證。

App 偏好設定

通知設定、類別排序等使用者偏好，存於裝置本機 UserDefaults，不離開您的裝置。

3. 兒童資料保護

家小事的使用者為家長，孩子的身高體重、學校繳費、才藝課等資訊由家長代為輸入與管理。本 app 並非設計給兒童直接使用。

  • 本 app 不直接向兒童收集任何個人資訊
  • 所有兒童相關資料僅存於家長的 iCloud 私有資料庫，不外傳
  • 本 app 不含廣告、不含第三方追蹤 SDK
  • 本 app 符合美國 COPPA（兒童線上隱私保護法）之精神：不向 13 歲以下兒童直接收集個人資料

4. 資料儲存與同步

本 app 使用 Apple 的 CloudKit 技術進行跨裝置同步。您的資料儲存於 Apple 管理的私有資料庫（Private Database），開發者無 Dashboard 可查看您的個人內容。

資料的隱私保護受 Apple 隱私政策規範：apple.com/legal/privacy

5. 第三方分享

我們不與任何第三方分享您的個人資料。本 app 不含廣告網路、分析 SDK、或社群媒體追蹤器。

6. 您的資料控制權

  • 刪除資料：您可在 app 設定頁選擇「清除本機所有資料」，或直接在 iPhone 設定 → 帳號 → iCloud → 管理儲存空間中刪除家小事的雲端資料。
  • 帳密保險箱：您可在 app 內逐筆刪除，密碼將同步從 Keychain 中移除。

7. 聯絡我們

如有任何隱私相關問題，請透過 App Store 的開發者聯絡方式與我們聯繫。

Famly does not collect or transmit any user data to developer servers. All data is stored only on your device and in your personal iCloud private database.

1. Overview

Famly (家小事) is a family record management app that helps parents track health records, vehicle maintenance, document expiry, pets, and children's growth information. This app is published by an independent developer.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information.

2. What data do we collect?

We collect no data. Everything you enter in the app is stored on your own device or in your personal Apple iCloud private database, which the developer cannot access.
Family Records

Health records, vehicle maintenance logs, document expiry dates, children's growth data, and pet information are synced through Apple's CloudKit private database and accessible only to you.

Password Vault

Passwords are stored in the device's iOS Keychain (hardware-encrypted) or synced via iCloud Keychain to your family members. Passwords are never uploaded to any third-party server. Viewing passwords requires Face ID / Touch ID authentication.

App Preferences

Notification settings, category ordering, and other preferences are stored locally in UserDefaults and never leave your device.

3. Children's Data

Famly is designed for parents. Information such as a child's height, weight, school payments, and activity classes is entered and managed by parents on behalf of their children. This app is not designed for direct use by children.

  • This app does not directly collect personal information from children
  • All children-related data is stored only in the parent's private iCloud database and is never shared externally
  • This app contains no advertisements and no third-party tracking SDKs
  • This app complies with the spirit of COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act): we do not directly collect personal data from children under 13

4. Data Storage & Sync

This app uses Apple's CloudKit technology for cross-device synchronization. Your data is stored in Apple's Private Database — the developer has no dashboard access to your personal content.

Data privacy is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy: apple.com/legal/privacy

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties. This app contains no ad networks, analytics SDKs, or social media trackers.

6. Your Data Controls

  • Delete data: You can choose "Clear All Local Data" in the app settings, or delete Famly's cloud data via iPhone Settings → Account → iCloud → Manage Storage.
  • Password Vault: You can delete individual entries within the app; passwords will be simultaneously removed from Keychain.

7. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us through the developer contact information on the App Store.
